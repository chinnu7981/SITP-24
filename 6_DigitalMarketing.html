Digital Marketing

About

Digital marketing, also called online marketing, is the promotion of brands to connect with potential customers using the internet and other forms of digital communication. This includes not only email, social media, and web-based advertising, but also text and multimedia messages as a marketing channel.

  • Time Duration: 6 Weeks Workshop
  • Timeline: 12th June 2023 to 28th July 2023

What you will learn?

This comprehensive course on digital marketing will equip you with the knowledge and skills to plan, execute, and optimize successful online marketing campaigns. Whether you're a marketing professional, business owner, or aspiring digital marketer, this course will empower you to navigate the ever-evolving digital landscape and drive tangible results for your organization
