Product development

About

Product development typically refers to all stages involved in bringing a product from concept or idea through market release and beyond. In other words, product development incorporates a product’s entire journey.

  • Time Duration: 6 Weeks Workshop
  • Timeline: 12th June 2023 to 28th July 2023

What you will learn?

This course provides a deep understanding of the key principles, methodologies, and strategies involved in successfully developing and launching innovative products. Through a combination of theoretical knowledge, case studies, and practical exercises, students will gain the necessary skills to navigate the product development lifecycle effectively.
